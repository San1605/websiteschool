We Are Albuquerque Academy

We are a private, coeducational, college preparatory day school serving middle school and high school students in grades 6 through 12, located in Albuquerque, NM.

Academy Prepares

How are we preparing your student for college and life?

Tomorrow’s Leaders

Through Academics, athletics, community service, outdoor education and extracurricular activities, Chargers have ample opportunities to become leaders.

Making a Difference

Academy students in every grade participate in Community Service. In 2019-20, Chargers in grades 6-12 logged more than 22,000 hours of community service.

Strong Bonds that Last

During their time at Academy, students build extraordinary relationships with classmates and faculty members – relationships that last a lifetime.

Nationally Ranked
